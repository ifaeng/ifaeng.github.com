Knowledge Base

Welcome to the Igiehon Mathematics Tournament, where numbers take center stage and equations come to life! Proudly sponsored by the prestigious Igiehon Foundation for Academic Excellence, this annual event is more than just a competition—it's a celebration of intellect and creativity.

On November 13th, the Edo Innovation Hub in Benin City will become the arena for over 240 exceptional secondary students from 120 schools across Edo State. Prepare for a day packed with mind-stretching challenges, spirited competition, and plenty of excitement!

With the distinguished Engr. Osa Igiehon as our patron and the dynamic Mrs. Nosa Igiehon as chairperson, we guarantee an event that is both inspiring and exhilarating. The Math4Life Foundation will be managing the tournament, ensuring a fair and exciting opportunity for every participant to shine!

Igiehon Math Tournament 2024

Get ready to unleash your inner mathlete! The Igiehon Mathematics Tournament is back for its third electrifying year, and it’s bigger and better than ever! Sponsored by the illustrious Igiehon Foundation for Academic Excellence, this event promises to be a brain-bending bonanza that you won’t want to miss!

On November 13th, the Edo Innovation Hub in Benin City will turn into a stage for a numbers showdown, where over 240 secondary students from 120 schools across Edo State will compete for glory, bragging rights, and perhaps a few math-themed trophies. Who will rise to the top? Will it be the school with the sharpest minds, the most calculators, or maybe just the best snacks? Only time—and some serious number crunching—will tell!

And guess what? This year, we’re going live! That’s right, you can watch all the action unfold from the comfort of your couch. So grab your popcorn, tune in, and cheer for your favorite school as they tackle mind-boggling problems and showcase their mathematical prowess!
